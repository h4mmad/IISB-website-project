Welcome to IIS Buraidah

School Vision

Presently IISB is striving to be a well equipped learning centre to serve as a model for effective teaching and learning in a dynamic educational environment where students achieve their maximum educational and personal potential and gain a love of lifelong learning, and teachers excel in pedagogy, stimulating young minds and encouraging them to extend excellence beyond the classroom.

We work respectfully and collaboratively to empower our students through education, in a safe, caring and culturally responsive environment and inspire students to master intellectual, social, physical and emotional skills needed to be a successful human being.

IISB's Mission and Vision statements are based on some values which are meant for further development and growth. Values arise out of what we believe to be important about people, about community and about learning and knowledge. Values inform and shape how the school is organised, how people relate each other in school, as well as the content and processes of teaching and learning.
